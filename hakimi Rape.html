Achraf Hakimi Has Been Charged For Rape

The 24 Years Old Morocco national team and Paris Saint Germain Defender ACHRAF HAKIMI has been charged with rape on thursday in the Parisian Suburb. According to Hakimi's Lawyer ,Fanny Collin, Hakimi denied the accusation and his lawer said firmly that he hasdone nothing wrong. Hakimi has placed under the supervision and was banned from contacting the alleged rape victim.


A french Press agency reports seeing hakimi Training with other team mates on friday against their match with Nantes on Saturday
